Our Story

Ralph and Maria met through their church group. At the time, Maria was active in the youth group along with Ralph’s brothers where she would always be seen around the house hanging out. Ralph then joined the young adult group, Singles for Christ, where Maria also transitioned from youth group. It was during this time that Ralph and Maria took notice of each other and also got to know one another better.

Maria could hardly be missed with her loud personality and various hairstyles and fashionable outfits to match. Her infectious spirit, singing talent, hearty laugh, and best of all, her beautiful smile, easily drew Ralph’s interest and quickly soon after, his heart. They started talking thanks to the “Twilight” book series and found themselves spending a lot of time talking over the phone. Ralph decided to court Maria making it official by asking her parents for their permission.

Nine years later, after going through ups and downs in their relationship, Ralph and Maria are FINALLY getting married and have chosen to be wed on their 10-year dating anniversary.

Ralph and Maria feel truly blessed and grateful to have each other along with the loving support of their family and friends. They’re excited to move on to the next phase of their relationship and be surrounded by their loved ones to witness and celebrate their love. They look forward to sharing their special day with you!
